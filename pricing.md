# The Walking Jumbo Service Pricing Guidelines

Use this file to determine scope, pricing, and packaging for all client proposals generated by the AI model.

## Pricing Categories & Packages

### 1. Website & App Development (One-Time)
- **Landing page**: ₹8,000 – ₹12,000 (Average target: ₹10,000)
  - Details: Single-page design, WhatsApp chat integration, contact form, fully mobile optimized, delivered in 5–7 days.
  - Target audience: Promotions, events, coaching offers, single-offer landing pages.
  - Tag: Most popular
- **Business website**: ₹15,000 – ₹25,000 (Average target: ₹20,000)
  - Details: 4–6 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact), Google Maps embed, WhatsApp chat. On-page SEO ready. Delivered in 10–14 days.
  - Target audience: Restaurants, salons, clinics, real estate brokers, coaching centers.
- **Ecommerce store**: ₹30,000 – ₹55,000 (Average target: ₹42,500)
  - Details: Product catalog, cart, payments integration (Razorpay / PhonePe), inventory management panel. Delivered in 20–30 days.
  - Target audience: Boutiques, D2C brands, retail stores.
- **Custom / large portal**: ₹60,000 – ₹1,50,000+ (Average target: ₹1,05,000)
  - Details: Real estate listings portal, booking / appointment systems, custom CRM or lead management. Delivered in 30–60 days.
  - Target audience: Multi-branch businesses, educational institutions, custom SaaS/portals.

### 2. Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) Retainers (Monthly)
*All SEO retainers require a minimum commitment. Prices are per month + GST.*
- **Local SEO — starter**: ₹5,000 / month
  - Details: Up to 10 keywords (city-level), on-page optimization, monthly ranking report. Minimum 3 months.
  - Target audience: Salons, single-location restaurants, dental/medical clinics.
- **Local SEO — growth**: ₹8,000 – ₹12,000 / month (Average target: ₹10,000)
  - Details: Up to 25 keywords, competitor tracking, 2 blog/content pieces per month, backlink building. Minimum 3 months.
  - Target: Real estate brokers, coaching institutes, local service businesses.
  - Tag: Recommended
- **SEO — aggressive growth**: ₹15,000 – ₹25,000 / month (Average target: ₹20,000)
  - Details: 50+ keywords, multi-location targeting, 4 content pieces per month, technical SEO audits, Core Web Vitals optimization. Minimum 6 months.
  - Target: Ecommerce stores, large institutes, property developers.

### 3. Google My Business (GMB) Management
- **GMB setup only**: ₹2,500 one-time
  - Details: Full profile setup, category selection, photos upload, business hours, description, and profile verification.
  - Target: Businesses starting from scratch.
  - Tag: Best value
- **GMB management**: ₹3,500 – ₹5,000 / month (Average target: ₹4,250)
  - Details: 4 Google posts per month, review response management, photo updates, Q&A management, monthly performance reports. Minimum 3 months.

### 4. Bundled Packages (Setup One-Time + Monthly Retainer)
- **Starter pack**: ₹12,000 setup + ₹6,500 / month
  - Includes: Landing page + GMB setup + Starter SEO + Monthly report.
  - Target: Very small local businesses.
- **Growth pack**: ₹22,000 setup + ₹12,000 / month
  - Includes: Business website + GMB management + Local SEO growth (25 keywords, 2 blogs/mo) + Monthly report.
  - Target: Restaurants, brokers, clinics, salons, coaching institutes.
  - Tag: Most popular bundle
- **Scale pack**: ₹45,000 setup + ₹22,000 / month
  - Includes: Ecommerce or large site + Aggressive SEO (50+ keywords, 4 blogs/mo, backlinks) + GMB management.
  - Target: Ecommerce brands, real estate portals, large institutes.

## Pricing Rules
- Always quote prices in Indian Rupees (₹).
- Explicitly add "+ GST" to all prices quoted in proposals.
- Prioritize Bundled Packages when a customer needs multiple services (e.g. website + marketing/SEO).
- In the recommended investment section, recommend the items matching the rules above. For ranges, suggest the average price unless the customer requirements dictate lower/higher ends.
